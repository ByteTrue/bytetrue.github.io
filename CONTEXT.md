# Personal Website

A personal website that represents the owner, featuring a simple home page (profile), a markdown-based blog (posts), and a showcase of projects.

## Language

**Owner**:
The individual whom this website represents. All content, bio, and projects belong to the Owner.
_Avoid_: User, author, admin

**Profile**:
The core information introducing the Owner, including name, role, bio, and social links.
_Avoid_: About, landing page, intro

**Post**:
A single markdown-based written article published by the Owner.
_Avoid_: Article, blog, diary, entry

**Project**:
A single showcase item representing the Owner's work, containing metadata and external links.
_Avoid_: Portfolio item, showcase, product

**Tech Democratization**:
The core philosophy of the Owner, focusing on lowering the entry barriers of complex, high-ceiling innovations so that they become accessible, stable, and practical for everyone.
_Avoid_: Tech populism, pure simplification
